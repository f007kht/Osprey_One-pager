NCT05006404 | Sponsored by Autus Valve Technologies, Inc.

Overview Progress & Timeline Eligibility Locations

Study Overview

This is a prospective, single-arm, multi-center pivotal study designed to evaluate the safety and effectiveness of the Autus Size-Adjustable Valve. This innovative medical device is intended for pediatric patients requiring surgical pulmonary valve replacement. The key feature of the Autus Valve is its ability to be expanded via a minimally-invasive catheter procedure to accommodate a child's growth, potentially reducing the need for repeated open-heart surgeries.

Participant Eligibility

To be considered for this study, potential participants must meet specific criteria. Below is a summary of the key requirements. Please consult with the study coordinators at the listed locations for a complete evaluation.

Age Range

2 to 21 Years

Sex

All

  • Recommended for surgical pulmonary valve replacement.
  • Has a native or repaired right ventricular outflow tract.
  • Body size suitable for implantation of a study device.
  • One of the following findings: Severe pulmonary stenosis, Moderate or greater pulmonary regurgitation, or a combination of both.
  • Willingness to provide informed written consent.
  • Requires valve replacement in a non-pulmonary position.
  • Has a prosthetic valve at another valve position.
  • Has pulmonary arterial hypertension.
  • Has an active infection requiring systemic antibiotic therapy.
  • Has active endocarditis.
  • Known hypersensitivity to anticoagulants and antiplatelet drugs.
  • Participating in another investigational study of a new drug or device.
