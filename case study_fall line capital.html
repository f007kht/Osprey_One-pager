Case Study: Fall Line Capital

Client Background

Fall Line Capital (FLC) is a private equity firm focused on investments in farmland and agricultural technologies. Their investment strategy requires deep domain expertise and thesis-driven research to identify and diligence opportunities across the ag-tech landscape.

Project Details

For FLC, Osprey Intel developed a series of sector-oriented research modules under stealth. This engagement highlighted our ability to align closely with a fund's specific investment thesis. The project involved mapping technology trends and competitive dynamics in a niche sub-sector, providing FLC's deal team with actionable intelligence to support their sourcing and screening activities.
