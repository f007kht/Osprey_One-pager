Case Study: Broadview Ventures

Client Background

Broadview Ventures is a mission-driven investment organization focused on funding the development of promising technology in cardiovascular and neurovascular disease. As a leading early-stage investor, they require deep, evidence-based diligence to support their portfolio companies.

Project Details

Osprey Intel executed a two-sprint flagship program for Broadview. The engagement focused on the pediatric pulmonary valve (PPV) market, a key area of interest for congenital heart disease (CHD) innovations. The primary objective was to rapidly generate an investor-grade market map, competitive landscape, and technology assessment to inform a time-sensitive investment decision.

Sources Cited

This engagement synthesized data from licensed sources including PitchBook, GlobalData, and various clinical trial registries, alongside public filings and textual analysis of KOL interview transcripts. All data points were linked to their source in the final deliverables for full auditability.
