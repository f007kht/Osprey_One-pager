Osprey Deep Dive — 6-module static report (PDF/HTML)

A concise, heavily cited research pack designed to get an investment team to "IC-ready" quickly.

Deal screening Sector brief Board/IC prep
Core modules
  1. Investor & Deal Activity — funding trends, active investors, and recent transactions.
  2. Company Financials & Business Models — revenue models, unit economics, pathways to scale.
  3. Technology & IP Landscape — modality map, patents, and defensibility signals.
  4. Market Sizing & Forecasts — explicit assumptions, scenarios, and sensitivity bands.
  5. Regulatory & Policy Context — pathways, guidances, and post-market dynamics.
  6. Strategic Positioning & White Space — comp map, archetypes, and where to play.

Osprey TalonStrike — interactive micro-app suite

A clickable, source-linked decision app. Ships with the Deep Dive core pack, plus interactive analytics modules.

Clickable Cited Explainable Audit-ready
Included modules
  • Deep Dive Core Pack — the six modules at left, rendered as interactive pages.
  • Comparables (Comps) — dynamic comp set with filters, KPIs, and notes.
  • Benchmarking — peer benchmarks for clinical, commercial, and financial metrics.
  • Market Sizing — Top-Down — adjustable assumptions and instant scenarioing.
  • Market Sizing — Bottom-Up — cohort/flow models with exposed inputs.
  • Company Profiles — individual deep dives (e.g., Autus Valve) with linked evidence.
  • Consolidated Shell & Index — a client-friendly control panel that ties it all together.
  • References & VC Toolkit — full citation trail and reusable investor artifacts.
